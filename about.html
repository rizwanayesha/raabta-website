Communication is a basic human right

Raabta was created to ensure that no person is excluded from education, healthcare, relationships, or opportunity simply because they communicate differently.

The Problem

Over 1 in 5 people worldwide experience communication challenges due to conditions such as autism, stroke, cerebral palsy, hearing impairments, or developmental delays.

Existing AAC tools are often expensive, complex, language-limited, and culturally disconnected. Many families and therapists struggle to find solutions that are affordable, intuitive, and adaptable to real-life situations.

The result? Individuals are unable to express pain, emotions, needs, or thoughts — not because they lack intelligence, but because they lack a voice.

The Solution — Raabta

Raabta is an AI-enabled assistive communication platform that transforms how people with speech impairments interact with the world.

  • Build sentences using simple tap-based symbols
  • Convert drawings and gestures into speech
  • Customizable communication boards for daily routines
  • AI-powered sign language assistant
  • Culturally and linguistically relevant design

Instead of forcing users to adapt to technology, Raabta adapts to the user.

Our Vision

We envision a world where communication differences never limit a person’s independence, dignity, or participation in society.

Raabta aims to become a global communication bridge used in classrooms, homes, hospitals, workplaces, and emergency settings. Enabling the speech deficit community to express themselves confidently everywhere.

Giving voice to every expression.
